Fund Request for $CATVAX Project

Overview

We are thrilled to announce the development of our new project website, the expansion of our social media presence, and the planning of significant global cat clinic donations. These initiatives aim to make $CATVAX more global.

Mission

Our mission is to enhance cats' health globally by improving access to veterinary care, raising awareness about cat health issues, and creating a strong online community of cat lovers and supporters.

Goals

  1. Website Development: Create an informative and user-friendly website to serve as a hub for our activities, providing resources, updates, and opportunities for community engagement.
  2. Social Media Expansion: Secure subscriptions for Premium social accounts and build a robust social media presence to reach a broader audience.
  3. Global Cat Clinic Donations: Organize and execute large-scale donations to cat clinics around the world.
  4. University Partnerships: Establish partnerships with universities that research serious health problems and diseases affecting cats.

Financial Needs

To achieve these goals, we need financial support to:

  • Develop and maintain our website.
  • Manage and grow our social media channels.
  • Coordinate and execute global donation campaigns.
  • Cover operational costs such as incentives to mods.

How You Can Help
